## Long description (200–400 words)

Roomward is hotel operations management software that connects the people who *see*
problems with the people who *fix* them. In most hotels, the staff who spot issues —
housekeepers, front desk — aren't the ones who repair them, and that hand-off is where
time, money, and guest satisfaction leak out. Roomward closes that gap.

When a housekeeper flags a leak, it instantly becomes a tracked work order. Maintenance
sees it, the front desk knows the room is down before they sell it, and nothing gets lost
between shifts. Every room lives on a real top-down floor plan, color-coded by status and
updating in real time.

**What Roomward does:**

- **Work orders** — log, assign, photo-document, and close maintenance with full history and priorities.
- **Live housekeeping board** — dirty → cleaning → ready, visible to the front desk the moment a room is done.
- **Preventive maintenance** — recurring tasks that schedule themselves so equipment is serviced before it fails.
- **Asset tracking** — a registry of every HVAC unit, water heater, and elevator, with service history.
- **Floor plans** — map any property top-down; see every room's status at a glance.
- **Roles & permissions** — front desk, housekeeping, maintenance, and managers each see exactly what they need.
- **Team chat** — coordinate in real time, encrypted, organized by channel.
- **PMS sync** — works alongside RoomMaster and Eptura rather than replacing them.

Roomward is priced per property, not per seat — so you can add your whole housekeeping
team without worrying about license counts. It's built for hotels, lodges, parks, and
similar facilities, by a team in North Georgia.

Start a free 14-day trial at roomward.app — no credit card required.
